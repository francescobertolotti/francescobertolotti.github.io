Music

I am playing the keyboard in rock and indie bands since I was a teenager. I am still doing it, sometimes. Music is and always will be a big part of my life. Sometimes, I even write about it. There is a good chance human civilization had reach its peak in the time lapse between Sgt. Peppers and Selling England by the Pound. Some day, I may write a book about the 100 songs that changed my life.
